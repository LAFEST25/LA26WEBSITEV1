RULES
GENERAL RULES
  • Registration is free of charge and limited to students of Classes IX to XII from participating schools.
  • The registration forms should be submitted by 13 June, 2026.
  • Participants are required to report by 7:00 a.m. on the day of the fest.
  • A maximum of 70 non-participating attendees are permitted per school.
  • Each registered school must enter at least 3 events. Each student is limited to a maximum of 2 events.
  • All attendees, irrespective of participation status, must wear their school uniforms and display their LA Fest ID cards at all times. Badges will be issued at the registration desk.
  • Entry is strictly restricted to registered students and accompanying teachers.
  • Two green rooms will be allotted to each school for preparation and storage.
  • Food coupons are priced at ₹350 each and are non-refundable.
  • All food orders, including vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences, must be finalised by 13 June, 2026. Changes will not be permitted thereafter.
  • The use of professional cameras within the indoor stadium is strictly prohibited. While mobile photography is permitted for personal use, such footage will not be accepted by the judges as evidence for disqualification.
  • Choreographers will not be permitted entry into the indoor stadium.
  • Food and beverages are not permitted inside the indoor stadium under any circumstances.
  • The decisions of the judges shall be final and binding.
  • Participants are expected to maintain the highest standards of sportsmanship and fair play. Usage of vulgar language, obscene actions, or disruptive behaviour will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Any use of vulgar language, obscene actions, or disruptive conduct by attendees will not be tolerated.
